Welcome to SR Sensory Gym

We see different abilities.

SR Sensory Gym is a safe, inclusive, and engaging sensory-based play space designed for neurotypical and neurodivergent children. Every child deserves a place where they feel understood, empowered, and celebrated.

Active sensory room

Children can jump, climb, and swing in a movement-friendly space designed for sensory seekers and children who may feel over or under stimulated.

Calming room

A relaxing space with soothing music, gentle lighting, and weighted products that can help children pause, refocus, and regulate.

Party room

A welcoming space for hosted birthday parties, celebrations, and kids' days out with family and friends.

Inside the lounge

A bright space built for movement, play, and calm.

Families can see the sensory gym before they arrive: active play zones, learning corners, calming tools, and colorful equipment designed for children to explore safely.

A space to explore and grow

Purposeful play in a judgment-free setting.

From climbing structures and crash pads to calming sensory areas and interactive activities, every part of the gym is designed to encourage development in a fun, supportive environment.

More than a gym

SR Sensory Gym is a welcoming community space where children of all abilities can explore, learn, and grow together. Our gym supports children with sensory processing needs, those on the autism spectrum, and children who benefit from movement-based and interactive play.

Sensory regulation Movement and calming spaces help children engage with new stimuli in a supportive environment.
Motor development Climbing, swinging, and active play give children room to move with purpose and build confidence.
Peer connection Structured and open-play experiences support social interaction in an inclusive setting.

Our mission

We exist to create a balance between neurotypical and neurodivergent children. It is our commitment and passion to provide a safe environment where all children can thrive and flourish.

Inclusion matters Every child deserves a place where they feel empowered and celebrated.
Community matters Families deserve a supportive space built around dignity, service, and connection.
Growth matters Purposeful play can support independence, resilience, and confidence over time.
